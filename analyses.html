Data Analyses

Research Question:

Is the fatality of a shooting incident associated with the victim’s sex or race?

1. Association between fatality and sex

Method: Simple Logistic Regression

Hypotheses:

H0: There is no relationship between the independent variable (sex) and the response variable (fatality).

Ha: There is a relationship between the independent variable (sex) and the response variable (fatality).

summary(glm(data$fatal ~ data$sex, data = data, family = binomial))

## glm(formula = data$fatal ~ data$sex, family = binomial, data = data)

Interpretation:

Since the p-value is less than 0.05, we can conclude that there is a significant relationship between the victim’s sex and fatality of the shooting incident.

Compared to a female, the victim being a male increases the log odds of fatality by a factor of 0.3574 (or, would increase the odds of fatality by a factor of e0.3574=1.4296).
